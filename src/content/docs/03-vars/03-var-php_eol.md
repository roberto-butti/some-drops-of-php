## Portable end of line: `PHP_EOL`
If you need to echo output then you need a portable end of line. My suggestion is to use the constant `PHP_EOL` instead of `\n` or `\r\n`.

```php
echo "Title" . PHP_EOL . "Subtitle" . PHP_EOL;
```
